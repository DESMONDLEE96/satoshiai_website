Swim with the Whale:
Wallet tracking

Knowing your crypto direction better by riding the wave

Whale tracking involves monitoring the movements of large cryptocurrency holders, also known as “whales.” Tracing the rapid blockchain transactions, J is able to identify these individuals or entities that hold or trade significant amounts of cryptocurrencies. And best of all, the tracking is real-time.

Why tracking the whales? With a large number of tokens being traded or held, these activities would form the market trends and give significant impacts on the DeFi market. Hence, sighting of these whale behaviors can essentially provide insights for individual crypto traders to make informed trading decisions.
